  • An assessment is a group of questions and/or topics in Know It? play mode designed to test your understanding of the content that you have viewed prior to the assessment. A progress bar (Question X of X) displays under the question so you can tell where you are in the assessment. As you answer a question, a Continue button appears so you can move to the next question.
