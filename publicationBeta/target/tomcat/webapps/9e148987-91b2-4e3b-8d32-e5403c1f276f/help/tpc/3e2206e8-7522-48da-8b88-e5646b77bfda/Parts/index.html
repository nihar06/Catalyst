The Outline pane displays the content available on the left side of the Player. As there may be a lot of content, there are several methods available, listed below, to help you find the appropriate documents. 

  • Use the Navigation Controls
     
  • Resize the Outline
     
  • Hide/Show the Outline
     
  • Filter Content by Role
  • Perform a Search
  • Use the Applicable Option with Context-sensitive Content
