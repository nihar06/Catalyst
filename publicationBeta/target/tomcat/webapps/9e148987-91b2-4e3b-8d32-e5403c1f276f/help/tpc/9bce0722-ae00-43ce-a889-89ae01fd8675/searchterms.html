  • A Print Results link appears in the Know It? results window. You can click this link to print a record of these results. The printed output displays the topic name, the result (Pass or Fail), your score, the required score, and the date and time the report was printed. A Signature line appears on the report as well. A supervisor or training manager may need to sign this report to confirm that you completed the topic successfully.
