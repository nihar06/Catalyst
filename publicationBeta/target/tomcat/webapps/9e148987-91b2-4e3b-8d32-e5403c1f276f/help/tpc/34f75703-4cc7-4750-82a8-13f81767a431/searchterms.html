  • In Do It! mode, the available paths are listed as links in the Do It! window and may also have instructions for choosing an option. Clicking a link launches the appropriate path.
  • If the Player is launched from a Help button or a Help menu in an application, and the matching context occurs on multiple paths of a Decision frame, the Decision frame appears. This allows the user to choose the path appropriate to the task they are performing.
