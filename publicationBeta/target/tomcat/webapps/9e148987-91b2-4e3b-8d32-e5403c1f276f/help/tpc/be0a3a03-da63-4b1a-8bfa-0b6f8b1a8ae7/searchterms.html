  • The Player has a Share feature, which provides you with a URL. You can send this URL to others, who will then be able to launch the content.
  • The Share feature is available when the Player is launched:
  • as part of Usage Tracking mode within the Knowledge Center
  • in any modeless launch
  • using the Launch Player link in the Knowledge Center
  • The Share feature is NOT available when the Player is launched from:
  • a Knowledge Path in the Knowledge Center
  • an LMS
  • a mode-specific launch
  • play.exe (from a local file system instead of a server)
  • The Share feature is available from two locations:
  • In the main Player window, the Share link appears above the Concept pane.
  • While playing a topic, a Share option is available on the Actions menu.
  • Share Content from the Player Window
  • When available, the Share link appears above the Concept pane. If you share a topic, the user who receives the link will be able to select the appropriate play mode. If you share a section, the concept displays. If you share a question or assessment, the question or assessment displays.
  • The Share dialog box opened through the Share link in the Player window has a View Outline option. When this option is not selected (default), the link is directly to the
  • topic (or other outline item such as a module or question) in the Player. The recipient of the link can click the View outline link to show the item in its location in the outline.
  • When the View Outline option on the Share dialog box is selected, the link launches the Player with the item selected in the outline.
  • Roles that have been set on an outline item are not applicable. In other words, if you are viewing an outline item within a role, you can send it to someone who is not assigned that role and they will be able to view it.
  • Note:
  • To share content with the Share link in the Player window:
  • Select the outline item you want to share.
  • Click the
  • Share
  • Click the
  • View Outline
  • Copy the URL that appears in the dialog box.
  • Paste the URL in an email or instant message.
  • Share Content from the Actions Menu During Playback
  • When available, the Share option appears on the Actions menu during playback in all modes. The link displays a Share dialog box with the URl and three additional options:
  • Start at the outline with this topic selected: The link opens the Player window with the current topic selected in the outline.
  • Start at the beginning of this topic: The link opens the topic in the same play mode and starts at the beginning of the topic.
  • Start this topic at the current location: The link opens the topic in the same play mode and begins at the current frame.
  • Note:
  • To share content with the Share option on the Actions link while playing a topic:
  • Select a topic in the Player outline and start it in any play mode.
  • On any frame, click the
  • Actions
  • Share
  • Select the share options as desired.
  • Copy and paste the URL link into an email or instant message.
