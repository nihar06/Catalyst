The Oracle Home page is displayed.


The Contract Management window is displayed.


The Revenue page is displayed.


Click the Tasks button.

The Tasks list is displayed.


In the Process section, click the Generate Revenue link.

The Generate Revenue page is displayed.


To update the To Date click the To Date field.

Click the Clear Date button.

Enter the appropriate date into the To Date field.


In this example, enter "08/23/17".

Click the scrollbar to navigate to additional fields.

Click the scrollbar to navigate to the Submit button.

Click the Submit button.

A Confirmation message window is displayed to indicate that the process was submitted.


Click the OK button.

Update any additional fields as needed.

Revenue has been successfully generated.
