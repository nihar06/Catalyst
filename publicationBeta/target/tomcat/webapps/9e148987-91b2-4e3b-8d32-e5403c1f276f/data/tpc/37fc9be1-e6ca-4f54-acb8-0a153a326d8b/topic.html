In the Capitalize Assets section, click the Create and Assign Assets in Excel link.

To continue, click the Yes button.

The Internet Explorer window is displayed to indicate what action to take with the file.


To open the file, click the Open button.

The Connect window is displayed.


To connect to the URL, click the Yes button.

The Login page is displayed.


Enter the appropriate ID and password inot the User ID and Password fields, then click the Sign In button.

The Create and Assign Assets spreadsheet is displayed.


To enter the project name, click the Project Name field.

Enter the appropriate name into the Project Name field.


In this example, enter "Capital 10001".

Enter the appropriate name into the Asset Name field.


In this example, enter "HP Computers".

Enter the appropriate description into the Description field.


In this example, enter "HP Computers".

In this example, Click the Estimated.

Click the scrollbar to navigate to the Book field.

Click the Book field.

Select the appropriate book from the drop-down.


In this example, Click the US CORPORATE BOOK.

Click the scrollbar to navigate to the Currency field.

Click the Currency field.

Click the scrollbar to navigate to the appropriate currency  from the drop-down.
