The Oracle Home page is displayed.

The Contracts page is displayed.


To expand the Search region, click the Expand Search button.

Click the scrollbar to navigate to the Contract Lists section.

To search for the contract by contract number, click the Number field.

Enter the appropriate contract number into the Number field.


In this example, enter "GE002".

Click to select the appropriate status checkboxes as necessary.


To include all statuses, click the All checkbox.


In this example, click the All checkbox.

Click the scrollbar to navigate to the Search button.

To execute the search based on the criteria entered, click the Search button.

Contracts matching the search criteria are listed.


Click to select the appropriate contract number link.


In this example, click GE002.

The corresponding Edit Contract: Overview page is displayed.


The Billing tab is displayed.


In the Revenue Plans section, click the Actions menu.

The Create Revenue Plan page is displayed.


Enter the appropriate name into the Name field.


In this example, enter "Rate Based Std Revenue Plan".

Click to select the appropriate method name.


In this example, click As Billed.

Click the Save Menu.

The Edit Contract page is displayed.


Click the Save menu.

The revenue plan has been successfully created.
